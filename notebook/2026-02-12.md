# 2026-02-12

## Objective
Machine shop consultation and chassis planning.

## Record of what was done
- Met with the machine shop staff to discuss the mechanical build.
- Decided to use a chassis from a previous semester's senior design project as a starting point to save fabrication time and material costs.
- Discussed position sensing and navigation logic.
- Decided to remove the physical stop sensors used by the previous group.
- Proposed hardcoding a coordinate grid into the MCU to govern the robot's movement across the panels.
- Suggested using Hall effect encoders to count the number of motor turns, allowing the MCU to track its position across the predefined grid accurately.

## Notes
- The previous group's chassis used a 2-axis gantry similar to a 3D printer (horizontal rail at top, two vertical rails, cleaning module slung between).
- We will keep the rail/pulley mechanical layout but replace the electronics package entirely.
- Hall effect encoders may be replaced with pure open-loop step counting later if budget or PCB space gets tight.
