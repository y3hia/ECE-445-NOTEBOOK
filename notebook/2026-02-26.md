# 2026-02-26

## Objective
Submit first-round PCB order.

## Record of what was done
- Finalized the routing and submitted the PCB design.

### Strategic Exclusions
We intentionally excluded the MPPT buck converter components from this first board. We will use the ECE 469 power board for all initial testing to verify the Perturb and Observe algorithm first.

### Late Catch
- Pointed out a major flaw in the sensor layout: placing the ambient light sensors directly on the PCB wouldn't work because the board will be housed inside an opaque 3D-printed enclosure.

### Solution
Removed the light sensors from the board and added 2-pin connector headers so we can mount the sensors externally on the chassis and run wires back to the MCU.

## Reflection
This is the kind of mistake that's easy to make when you're zoomed into the schematic and not thinking about the physical assembly. Good catch by the team during the final review.
